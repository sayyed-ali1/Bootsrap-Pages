ZONE DEVELOPER

Transforming regions into thriving hubs of industry, trade, and technology.

WHO SHOULD APPLY FOR A ZONE DEVELOPER LICENSE?

The STZA Zone Developer License is designed for public, private, or public-private entities developing, operating, or managing technology-led infrastructure that contributes to Pakistan’s innovation economy.

This license is ideal for:

Technology parks and campuses that attract multiple Zone Enterprises
that provide office or operational space to multiple tech companies, startups, or research units.
Dedicated single enterprise zones for manufacturing, assembly, or innovation
(e.g., EV production, electronics plants, data centers)

STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES OF THE LICENSE

Unlock sustainable demand from Pakistan’s growing technology sector, reduce capital expenditure through tax incentives, and repatriate profits with complete freedom. The STZA Zone Developer License is designed to maximize return on investment.

10-year exemptions on all income taxes, custom duties on capital goods, and more.
Restriction-free capital flow through Exclusive Special Forex Accounts
Fully repatriable profits and dividends (subject to local taxes)
100% business ownership for foreign and local investors
Increased demand from the Technology Sector due to Special Technology Zone status
Digitized licensing through the STZA One Window Portal

GET YOUR LICENSE DIGITALLY IN 4-EASY STEPS

A step by step guide on the process to avail your license for STZA as a Zone Developer in 4 Easy Steps.

Prepare Your Documents

Zone Enterprises are assessed on contributions such as R&D, job creation, human capital development, technology transfer, exports, and innovation. Applicants must provide supporting documentation for claims made.

Submit Application Digitally

Apply digitally through the STZA One Window Portal and upload all required documents online.

Review & Evaluation

STZA reviews applications for compliance and alignment with sectoral priorities.

License Issuance

Successful applicants receive the Zone Enterprise License - enabling access to incentives and ecosystem access.
