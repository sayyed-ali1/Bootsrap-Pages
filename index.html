About STZA

Driving growth through Triple-helix model of innovation

The Special Technology Zones Authority (STZA) is a Federal Government body mandated to build a globally competitive knowledge and technology ecosystem in Pakistan. STZA enables the development of Special Technology Zones (STZs) by licensing technology enterprises, infrastructure developers, and academic institutions. Operating within these ring-fenced zones, the ecosystem players benefit from tax and custom holidays, stable policy and world-class technology infrastructure that fosters innovation, attracts capital, and drives sustainable growth in the technology sector.
