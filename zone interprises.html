Scale your Technology Business with STZA’s Zone Enterprise licensing framework

Who Should Apply for a Zone Developer License

Tech startups, SaaS Companies, Fintech Sector, Advanced production companies, or technology services Exporters. With the Zone Enterprise license, STZA empowers technology companies to scale globally through Fiscal and Forex incentives, infrastructure, and access to a thriving ecosystem.

Eligibility Criteria for The Zone Enterprise License

Have a legal entity in Pakistan registered with Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP)
Have a space or land allotted in one of the notified Special Tech Zones
Have a business in one of the technology categories under the STZA (Qualification and Approval) Rules 2021

GET YOUR LICENSE DIGITALLY IN 4-EASY STEPS

A step by step guide on the process to avail your license for STZA as a Zone Developer in 4 Easy Steps.

Prepare Your Documents

Zone Enterprises are assessed on contributions such as R&D, job creation, human capital development, technology transfer, exports, and innovation. Applicants must provide supporting documentation for claims made.

Submit Application Digitally

Apply digitally through the STZA One Window Portal and upload all required documents online.

Review & Evaluation

STZA reviews applications for compliance and alignment with sectoral priorities.

License Issuance

Successful applicants receive the Zone Enterprise License - enabling access to incentives and ecosystem access.
