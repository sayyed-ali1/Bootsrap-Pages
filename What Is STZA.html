WHAT IS STZA?

A Federal Government body mandated to build a globally competitive knowledge and technology ecosystem in Pakistan

Autonomous body established under Cabinet Division.

Mandated by the Act of Parliament (6th October, 2021)

Prime Minister as the President of STZA’s Board of Governors (BoG).

Board of Governors composition: Government & international private sector leaders..

Facilitator & regulator for public, private and public–private technology zones in all parts of Pakistan.

OUR MANDATE

Approve, Notify, and License Zones & Zone Entities

Develop independent Rules and Regulations for Special Technology Zones

Provide fiscal and forex incentives for the period of 10 years to Zone Entities

Enable development of Pakistan’s Tech Ecosystem

Our Vision

Enable a globally competitive knowledge and technology ecosystem in Pakistan

Our Missions

Facilitate and regulate the development of world-class public, private, and public-private Special Technology Zones to improve the country’s global competitiveness. Enhance ease of doing business and cost effectiveness for local and global technology companies in STZs to boost investments, innovation, technology development, job creation, import substitution, and tech exports.

STZA and the Triple Helix Model of Innovation

The Special Technology Zones Authority (STZA) is built on the globally recognized Triple Helix Model of Innovation, which emphasizes synergy between industry, academia, and government to drive sustainable technological growth.
