LEADERSHIP

Guided by vision and integrity, our leadership drives innovation, transparency, and community-focused progress.

Prime Minister’s Message

We are committed to the vision of Pakistan becoming a leading global destination for technology investments. STZA is the cornerstone of our technology strategy laying foundations towards building a world class knowledge ecosystem for local and global technology stakeholders, and scale their operations in Pakistan to service the global tech markets.

Mr. Muhammad Shehbaz Sharif

President of STZA Board of Governors

Chairman’s Message

We have embarked on enabling the development of a world-class technology ecosystem in Pakistan for global and local technology stakeholders. STZA offers a seamless business environment for investors, reliable technology infrastructure, 10 years of fiscal and forex incentives, and a thriving tech ecosystem to unlock Pakistan’s demographic and knowledge advantages.

Azfar Manzoor

Chairman of STZA
